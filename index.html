Arizona Relativity

Black Holes • Strong Gravity • Relativistic Plasmas

We build predictive models of extreme environments using numerical relativity, GRMHD, and radiative transfer.

Numerical Relativity

We evolve Einstein’s equations in 3+1D to model mergers and dynamical spacetimes.

GRMHD

Relativistic magnetized plasmas around BHs: accretion, jets, and flares.

Multimessenger

We connect gravitational waves, EM radiation, and neutrinos with predictive models.
